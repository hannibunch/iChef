Instruction

Step 01

Prepare the Zucchini

Slice zucchinis lengthwise. Lay the zucchini slices on a paper towel and sprinkle with salt. Let them sit for 10-15 minutes to draw out excess moisture. Pat dry with another paper towel.

Feel free to sprinkle some fresh herbs like parsley or thyme before serving for an extra burst of freshness and aroma!
